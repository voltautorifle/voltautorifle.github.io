Bycicles

The first bike shown is a road bike that is made to be rode on paved roads. This particular bike comes in varying sizes and is a Windsor Wellington 3.0 that has an aerodynamic aluminum frame with steel chains and stainless steel spokes. The second bike is a hybrid that is meant to be able to go anywhere. It's performance is not as good as the specialised ones, but you don't need to worry about it unless you do some extreme stuff. This particular model is a 700c Schwinn DSB that has a rigid aluminum frame, double alloy rims and a tool free adjustable seat (this bike is meant for people from 5'4" to 6'2"). The third bike is a mountain bike that is meant to be used off road. This one is a Diamondback Recoil 29er with a full suspension aluminum frame, DB SL7 doublewall alloy wheels and Tektro Areis mechanic brakes.
