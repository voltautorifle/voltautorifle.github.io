The Cycle the Continent Divide annual event starts March first. We meet at our shop and skirt the Idaho border then go through Wyoming to the bottom of New Mexico. It has a cost of $190 per person. All your basic needs are provided for so all you need to bring is anything extra you might need (and a water bottle if you want to get a drink during the trip).
